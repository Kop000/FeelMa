The neuroanatomy of developmental language disorder: a systematic review and meta-analysis

贡献

发现了与 DLD 相关的前新纹状体异常，深入了解所涉及的特定大脑区域 。

结果

Structural neuroanatomical abnormalities

  • Level 1 : basal ganglia, frontal cortex
  • Level 2 : neostriatum(a key structure in the basal ganglia)

Functional neuroanatomical abnormalities

  • Level 1 : basal ganglia, frontal cortex
  • Level 2 : Inferior parietal, Globus pallidus
方法

  • “ we employed a recently developed anatomical metaanalytic approach, co-localization likelihood estimation (CLE）”

  • “ Thus, it can include a much larger and more heterogeneous set of studies, increasing the reliability and generalizability of the results. “

  1. Simply calculate the proportion (weighted by study sample size)
  2. computed the permutation-based likelihood (the observed rate of anomalies was non-random)
  3. predicts abnormalities in the basal ganglia, in particular the neostriatum
    (based on the procedural circuit deficit hypothesis (PDH))
数据

  • structural neuroanatomy of DLD: 22 papers(16 unique participant groups, 577 unique participants)

  • functional imaging papers: 11 (12 unique participant groups, 414 unique participants )

背景
  • 论文背景: 发育性语言障碍是一种常见的神经发育障碍，其神经基础尚不清楚。
  • 过去方案: 以往的研究主要集中在传统的语言和听觉区域，如布洛卡区和颞上皮质，而对发育性语言障碍的神经解剖学相关性了解较少。
  • 论文的Motivation: 本研究旨在通过系统性综述和荟聚分析揭示发育性语言障碍的神经解剖学特征，以填补这一领域的知识空白。
概要

本研究通过系统性识别和量化综合神经解剖学研究，使用最近发展的神经解剖学荟萃分析技术，揭示发育性语言障碍的神经解剖学特征，发现异常主要集中在基底神经节，具体是前纹状体。

荟萃分析是一种统计分析方法，用以将多个独立的临床研究结果综合起来进行定量分析。
